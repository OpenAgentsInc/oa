Open Agents

AI agents will soon outnumber humans. For true AI safety, a majority of agent code, data, weights should be as open & inspectable as possible.

One Market

We will liberate frontier AI from captured centralized corporations by building one decentralized global marketplace of AI agents and services.

Bitcoin Only

Anything of value in "crypto" will be absorbed into layers on, anchored to, or adjacent to Bitcoin.

Incentivized Interop

The agent-centric web will have its own network effect, rewarding those who make the web traversable to agents and punishing non-participants. Companies hiding behind walled gardens will not survive the transition to the agentic web.

Neutrality Wins

The future will be built on neutral protocols for money, data, & AI.
